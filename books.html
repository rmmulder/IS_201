BSA / AML Leadership & Expertise

Richard Mulder brings more than a decade of hands‑on leadership in BSA/AML compliance, designing and scaling AML programs that withstand regulatory scrutiny. He combines practical program governance, transaction monitoring strategy, and data‑driven risk management to build resilient, audit‑ready frameworks. As a leader he mentors compliance teams, aligns stakeholders, and delivers measurable improvements in monitoring, investigations, and operational controls.

About This Book

Endless regulations. Unrelenting scrutiny. In the high‑stakes world of Anti‑Money Laundering, success demands more than compliance — it requires strategy. Strategies for BSA Officers equips BSA Officers and AML professionals with a practical Plan, Build, and Refine methodology to develop adaptive, audit‑ready AML programs that reduce risk, improve monitoring, and secure executive buy‑in. Whether preparing for audits, optimizing transaction monitoring, or improving governance, this guide provides actionable steps to transform AML compliance into a strategic advantage.
